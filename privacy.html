Privacy Policy

MDGenius AI INC  ·  Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MDGenius AI INC ("MDGenius AI," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and our healthcare technology platform. MDGenius AI INC is located at 848 Brickell Avenue, PH 5, Miami, FL 33131. For any privacy question, contact us at privacy@mdgeniusai.com.

We handle two distinct categories of information under different rules: (1) information from website visitors and prospective patients who contact us for information, and (2) protected health information processed inside our platform on behalf of healthcare provider clients. This policy describes both.

1. Website Visitors and Prospective Patients

When you visit our website or submit a contact or information request, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and the details of your inquiry. This information is provided voluntarily by you to request information about procedures, pricing, scheduling, or our services. At this stage, before you enter into care with a provider, this information is treated as contact and business information, not as protected health information.

We use this information to respond to your request, provide the information you asked for, schedule consultations, and follow up regarding the services you inquired about.

2. SMS / Text Messaging and Mobile Information

When you provide your mobile phone number and opt in through our website (by checking the SMS consent box on our contact form) or by initiating a conversation with us, you consent to receive SMS text messages from MDGenius AI INC.

These messages are sent in response to your inquiry and may include information about procedures, pricing, consultation scheduling, appointment reminders, and related follow-up regarding the services you asked about. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out of SMS will not affect your ability to contact us by other means.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

3. Patient Information Within the Platform (Protected Health Information)

When you become a patient of a healthcare provider that uses the MDGenius AI platform and enter the surgical preparation or post-operative monitoring process, the information processed about you may include protected health information (PHI) under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

For this patient information, MDGenius AI INC acts as a Business Associate of the healthcare provider (the Covered Entity). We process PHI only as permitted under a Business Associate Agreement with the provider and applicable law, including for treatment support, care coordination, monitoring, and the services the provider has engaged us to perform. The provider's own notice of privacy practices governs the use and disclosure of your PHI.

We apply administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to PHI, including access controls, audit logging, and encryption of data in transit and at rest.

4. How We Protect Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect all information we handle, including encryption, access controls, and secure infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using appropriate safeguards.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share mobile information or SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information with service providers who help us operate (for example, secure hosting, messaging, and email delivery), bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations, and with healthcare providers as needed to deliver the services you or they have requested. We may disclose information where required by law.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.
  • Request access to or correction of your information by contacting us.
  • Unsubscribe from email communications using the link in our emails.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, to comply with legal obligations, and as required under agreements with healthcare provider clients.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from them through our website.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
